# System Usability Scale (SUS) Questionnaire

## Instructions

Rate each statement on a scale of 1-5 by replacing the underscore with your chosen number:
- **1** = Strongly Disagree
- **2** = Disagree  
- **3** = Neutral
- **4** = Agree
- **5** = Strongly Agree

**Example**: If you agree with a statement, replace the underscore with 4:
`Your rating: 4`
